Yes. You’ll be assigned to a dedicated learning coach.

We’ve partnered with The Learning Basket for your source of living books.

Not sure where or which book set to buy? Don’t worry, we’ll also direct you to where you can source and buy homeschool-related and DepEd-compliant resources.

Paano ang learning materials niyan sis? May guide ba? Then tayo na ang gagawa ng lessons?

Your learning coach will guide and discuss with you which approach works best for your child. They’ll also share where you can buy DepEd-compliant learning resources to maintain the quality of their lessons.

You can choose from two approaches:

    The Boxed Approach
    The Open Curriculum Approach

For the Boxed Approach, you can buy your child’s recommended and pre-selected learning resources from local and online stores.

With the Open Curriculum Approach, you’ll have full creativity in creating their lessons, with guidance and consultation from your Learning Coach.

No questions are too light or unimportant. Ask your learning coach about your homeschool concerns and they’ll make sure your child gets the most out of the approach you choose.

Gusto ko sana yung Open Approach kaso parang di ko kakayanin sa ngayon … Yung boxed naman all subjects yan sis? Then ako na buy ng books?

Yes, the Open Approach covers all subjects. And, your learning coach will lead you to where you can find your child's books. If you prefer buying pre-packaged learning materials, the Boxed Approach might work best for your situation.
